Key Engineering Constraints

  • Max forward speed: ~120 kts — hard limit imposed by ducted fan forward-flight drag
  • Coaxial disc separation: 1.0 – 1.5 m minimum for aerodynamic efficiency
  • Fan tip-to-duct clearance: Fan must fill ~95% of duct inner diameter
  • CG zone: 6.5 – 8.5 m from nose (fan offset geometry)
  • Hot/high margin (3 pax): Comfortable to ~6,000 ft / ISA +20°C before degradation
  • Water displacement: ~4–6 tonnes estimated (sealed hull volume)
  • Yaw control method: Differential RPM between coaxial upper/lower discs
  • Certification path: FAA experimental aircraft or Part 23/27 — determine early
